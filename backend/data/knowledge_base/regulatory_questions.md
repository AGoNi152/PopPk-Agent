# Common Regulatory Questions

Why was the selected exposure metric chosen for the exposure-response analysis?

How do the model diagnostics support the final PopPK model?

How was model uncertainty incorporated into simulations?

Does the simulated exposure range remain within the observed clinical exposure range?

How do you justify no dose adjustment in subgroups where exposure is lower or higher?

Is the exposure-response analysis confounded by disease severity, response status, time-varying clearance, or treatment discontinuation?

What evidence supports the claim that covariate effects are not clinically meaningful?

Is the target population represented by the analysis dataset and simulated population?

What evidence supports extrapolation to patients not well represented in the dataset?

What are the limitations of the model-informed dose recommendation?
