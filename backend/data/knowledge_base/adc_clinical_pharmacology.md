# ADC Clinical Pharmacology Optional Module

ADC clinical pharmacology packages often need to address total antibody, conjugated antibody or ADC, unconjugated payload, relevant catabolites, ADA, and NAb. Bioanalytical strategy should be aligned with linker stability, payload toxicity, DAR distribution, and mechanism of release.

FIH dose rationale for ADCs should integrate toxicology species selection, HNSTD or NOAEL, payload-related toxicity, target expression, clinical precedent, exposure margins, and dose escalation safeguards.

Exposure-safety analyses for ADCs should consider payload-related adverse events, hematologic toxicity, liver toxicity, ocular toxicity when relevant, and timing of toxicity relative to exposure metrics.
